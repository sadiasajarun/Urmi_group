URMI GROUP · DESIGN VARIATION SHOWCASE

Three takes on the homepage.

Three distinct visual directions for the Urmi Group homepage. All three honor the locked brand decisions (blue primary, Bengali wordmark, six crafts, sustainability as operating system). They differ in how — palette balance, type pairing, density, and motion language. Pick one — or call out a "Mix" — and we'll lock the choice in DESIGN_STATUS.md.

Each preview below is the live HTML scaled down. Open the standalone files for a full-size view: A-home.page.html · B-home.page.html · C-home.page.html · D-home.page.html

Variation D added 2026-05-07 in response to: "high cinematic but not dark navy blue dominant; soothing shade of green along with blue which represents sustainability and wave."

A

Editorial Wave

Open ↗

Polished magazine cover of a sustainable industrial group. Closest to the as-built React homepage. Mid-density layouts, Playfair Display + Inter, blue palette with sage sustainability accents, wave-clip section dividers.

  • · Hero: full-bleed video / blue gradient + soft overlays
  • · Pillars: 4 wave-clip-top cards with rise-in animation
  • · Best for: clients who want polish, not reinvention
  • · Implementation cost: low (matches current src/)
B

Atelier

Open ↗

Loro Piana with a Bengali soul. Sparse. Whitespace as luxury. Type-driven hero (no video). Bengali wordmark promoted to first-class display element. Sage and amber de-emphasized; ink hairlines replace ornament.

  • · Hero: type-driven, no video, no full-bleed photo
  • · Pillars: borderless type+stat blocks (no images)
  • · Best for: clients chasing quiet-luxury aesthetic
  • · Implementation cost: medium (3 wks of frontend rework)
C

Cinematic Mill

Open ↗

A film about a factory. Dark navy dominates. Heavy editorial photography across every section. Tiempos + IBM Plex Mono pairing. Brass accent replaces amber. Section transitions cross-fade like film cuts.

  • · Hero: cinematic photograph + brass frame
  • · Pillars: cream callouts on full-bleed photo strip
  • · Best for: clients who said "Brandix" and meant it
  • · Implementation cost: high (6–8 wks; needs photo budget)
D

Cinematic Tide

your synthesis
Open ↗

Brandix-tier cinematic vocabulary on a soft, sun-lit palette. Light backgrounds dominate; brand blue and lagoon green flow as twin currents (river + sustainability). Tiempos + Plex Mono type pairing kept from C, but warm wave-blue accent replaces brass.

  • · Hero: cinematic photo on warm gradient (no navy)
  • · Palette: blue + tide-green twin protagonists
  • · Best for: cinematic ambition without dark-mode heaviness
  • · Implementation cost: ~5–7 wks; survives placeholder photo better than C
